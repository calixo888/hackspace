Requirements


With HackSpace being a software development organization, there are certain requirements that all members must adhere to. General requirements are requirements that all members must follow, and the other requirements are rules for other types of roles within HackSpace. Members may take responsibility in multiple different roles, but must follow the unique rules set for that specific job.

General Requirements

  • Most convenient to be in high school
  • Able and willing to set aside time to in-person meetings, online conference meetings, and asynchronous messaging through WhatsApp/Slack
  • Quick to respond and help with projects
  • Experience and interest in computer science
  • Good team member to work and collaborate with
  • Able to work on current projects and tasks assigned multiple days

Developer

Developers specialize in programming and building the actual product being assigned. Developers must have a basic knowledge of computer software, operating systems, and command-line tools. Examples include: Using Git and GitHub for code organization, knowing and using basic terminal commands, using text editors and IDEs, and much more. Developers must have a strong communication with the rest of the development team and the project manager for efficiency and asynchronous flow.
  • Experience in multiple programming languages
  • Proficient in one programming language
  • Basic experience with Git and GitHub
  • General knowledge of computers
  • Understand the software development process
  • Works well in a team environment
  • Works well in a virtual/remote team

Designer

Designers specialize in graphic design, and more specifically, desiging logos, mapping out user interfaces, editing photos and/or videos, and editing website designs. Designers also help with video editing and must have experience with photo editing software, such as Adobe Photoshop.
  • Able to come up with original, unique designs
  • Experience with photo editing/creating software
  • Able to design logos, website layouts, and user interfaces
  • Work quickly in short periods of time

Business Analyst

Business analysts specialize in communicating with the client and the development team to come up with deadlines and micro-deadlines and to organize events so the product can be completed, tested, documented, and deployed on time. Business analysts also market and promote the product to advertise and expand HackSpace.
  • Good soft skills
  • Experience with Powerpoint/Google slides
  • Good at project management
  • Organized

Quality Assurance Developer

A quality assurance developer, or a QA Developer, specializes in testing and giving feedback based on what the specified client is looking for in their request, and addresses these changes directly to the project manager and to the development team. These developers also help with bug testing the actual product.
  • Work quickly in short periods of time
  • Pay close attention to what the client needs
