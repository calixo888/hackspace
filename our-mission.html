Our Mission

HackSpace is a software development organization comprised of high schoolers. We develop programs and products based on the personal request of the organization that we query. The clients that we target are other non-profit organizations such as Trivalley CoderDojo, Girls who Code, etc. We provide an experience for high schoolers that is not available anywhere else, giving high schoolers the opportunity to work on a real-world projects on a team of experienced and talented developers. We create our products following the software development process for an efficient, organized flow. If you enjoy programming and want to join an organization where you can work with others with similar interests on real-world projects, join us today through the contact us page!
